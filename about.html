Founded in 2010 by Chef Parker, Parker's Pizza has continued to serve the freshest pizzas in the bustling and fact-paced New York City.

Jean Parker, American-Italian, first started Parker's Pizzeria in 2010 to share his secret italian recipe to the world. Parker's pizzas give a timeless taste to the tongues and hearts of New Yorkers, as 14 years later, he continues to cook the highest quality pizzas in all of New York!
In the bustling New York City, we believe that every moment is precious. We crafted Parker's Pizzeria as a place where no moment or bite would go to waste!
